**Tropival** is a premium B2B platform connecting high-quality tropical produce from Africa (Ghana and Kenya) directly to European distributors and retailers via a strategic hub in Spain.

Built with a focus on visual excellence and seamless user experience, Tropival showcases a range of fresh produce, specialty crops, and premium snacks while telling the story of sustainable sourcing and market intelligence.

---

## âœ¨ Features

- **Interactive Product Catalog**: Detailed sections for Fresh Produce (Mangoes, Papayas, Passion Fruit) and Dried Snacks.
- **B2B Strategy & Insights**: Comprehensive market intelligence, opportunity analysis, and roadmap for European expansion.
- **Sourcing Transparency**: Follow the journey of produce from Ghanaian and Kenyan farms to the Spanish distribution hub.
- **Compliance & Quality**: Built-in sections documenting GAP, Smeta, and Global GAP certifications.
- **Custom Partnerships**: Dedicated B2B inquiry system for white-label and bulk supply partnerships.
